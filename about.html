About Us

✈️ Our Growth Story

From the lush landscapes of Kerala to the dynamic skyline of Dubai, Air Imarah is a story of passion, vision, and the relentless pursuit of excellence in travel.Our journey began with the founding of Air Emarat Travel & Tours LLP in Kerala, India, in 2023.

In a short time, Air Emarat built trust through personalized service, transparency, and customer- first values. To meet rising demand for global travel, we expanded—and in 2025, Air Imarah was born with its new headquarters in Dubai, a global hub of travel and innovation.

This marked a new chapter in our mission to connect people, cultures, and destinations through extraordinary journeys.At the core of Air Imarah is a deep commitment to personalized service, expert planning, and thoughtful attention to detail. From luxury getaways to cultural adventures, we turn your travel dreams into reality.Explore beyond the ordinary—embrace the journey, create unforgettable stories, and discover a world of wonder.

Mission & Vision

Defining our purpose and guiding our journey towards transformative travel experiences

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify and enhance every stage of the travel experience by offering expert visa assistance, reliable transport and transfers, curated tour packages, group and corporate travel planning, and comprehensive services like hotel booking, ticketing, visa stamping, and medical registration. We strive to be a one-stop travel partner committed to customer satisfaction, efficiency, and personalized service.

Our Vision

To become a trusted global leader in travel services by blending innovation with hospitality, ensuring every client enjoys a smooth, stress-free journey. We envision a future where travel is not only accessible and efficient but also empowering—connecting people to cultures, opportunities, and meaningful memories.
